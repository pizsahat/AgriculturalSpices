About Us

PT.IMSAJI was established to build a business engaged in food supply, especially spices products and their derivatives. Seeing the huge potential and growth of agricultural land productivity in Indonesia has encouraged us to empower farmers by opening up wider market access. We work with local farmers spread across most of Sumatra island to build a strong supply chain. Semi-traditional agriculture and fertile land produce quality products at relatively cheap prices compared to Java Island. We have a strong commitment to continue to innovate developing environmentally friendly agricultural systems to continue to be a food supplier solution.

At IMSAJI, we believe in providing a wide range of fresh and healthy products that all people's can enjoy. As Indonesian specialist wholesaler, we are the place to go for all the best fresh and healthy stock. From fresh fruits and vegetables to nutritious nuts and seeds and Spices
